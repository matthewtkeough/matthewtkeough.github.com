About Me

I am a graduate student at George Mason University, studying for my Masters in History with a concentration in Applied History and a specialization of late 19th Century American history. I work full time as an Administrative Assistant at the American Historical Association and volunteer at the the Arlington Historical Society as a docent coordinator. In what little free time I have I enjoy going out to dinner with my wife, watching Mad Men, and playing basketball.
